Top Marketing Channels for Mobile App Launch (2023)

  1. App Store Optimization (ASO) Free Channels

    Optimize title, keywords, screenshots, and ratings for organic discovery on iOS & Android.

  2. Apple Search Ads & Google App Campaigns Paid Search

    High-intent installs from store search and universal app campaigns.

  3. TikTok — Breakout Channel 2023 Key channel

    Short-form UGC, Spark Ads, and creator partnerships for viral reach.

  4. Meta Paid Social (Facebook / Instagram) Proven scale

    Lookalike audiences, Advantage+ app campaigns, and retargeting funnels.

  5. Influencer & Creator Marketing

    Micro-influencers for authenticity; trackable links and promo codes.

  6. Community, Referral & SEO

    Discord communities, referral rewards, and content SEO for long-tail growth.

Key 2023 Trends to Keep in Mind

  • Privacy-first attribution (SKAN, aggregated measurement)
  • Short-form video as primary creative format
  • AI-assisted creative testing at scale
  • Retention & LTV over raw install volume
